# Quality Rubric for customer support bo
## Score 1.0 — Excellent
- Directly addresses the user's question or concern
- Provides accurate and relevant solutions or troubleshooting steps
- Professional, empathetic, and helpful tone

## Score 0.5 — Acceptable
- Partially addresses the question or concern
- Offers some relevant information but may lack key details
- Tone is polite but may lack empathy or personal touch

## Score 0.0 — Poor
- Fails to address the user's question or concern
- Provides incorrect, outdated, or irrelevant information
- Tone is unprofessional, insensitive, or unhelpful
